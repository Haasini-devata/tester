The movie revolves around five women who team up with a struggling crime novelist, "Pencil" Parthasarathy (Nani), to hunt down the killer of their loved ones in a brutal bank heist.

  • A group of women, each having lost a loved one in the heist, join forces to seek justice.
  • They recruit Pencil, a plagiarizing crime novelist, to help them track down the killer, Dev (Kartikeya).
  • Dev, an F1 racer, had masterminded the heist with terminally ill cancer patients and killed them after getting the money.
  • The group uncovers Dev's past and hidden stash, but he tips off the police, leading to a series of twists.
  • In the end, Pencil kills Dev in self-defense, and the women decide to forgive and move on.
